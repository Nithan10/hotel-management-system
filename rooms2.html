Suite Details

Our Executive Suite redefines luxury living with over 750 square feet of elegantly designed space. This expansive suite features a separate living room, a lavish bedroom, and a dining area, all adorned with contemporary decor and premium finishes. The floor-to-ceiling windows offer breathtaking panoramic views of the city skyline, creating an unforgettable backdrop for your stay.

Designed for discerning travelers and business executives, the Executive Suite includes a dedicated workspace with high-speed internet, a conference table for meetings, and state-of-the-art technology. The marble-appointed bathroom features a luxurious Jacuzzi tub, a separate rain shower, and premium toiletries. The fully-equipped kitchenette allows for convenient in-suite dining, complemented by a premium minibar selection.
