Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common inquiries about our services and policies.

What is your cancellation policy?

We offer flexible cancellation up to 48 hours before arrival without any charges. For special packages and peak seasons, please refer to your booking confirmation for specific terms.

Do you offer airport transfer services?

Yes, we provide luxury airport transfers in our fleet of premium vehicles. Please inform us of your flight details at least 24 hours in advance, and our concierge will arrange everything for you.

Can you accommodate special dietary requirements?

Absolutely. Our culinary team is experienced in accommodating all dietary needs including vegan, gluten-free, kosher, halal, and any allergies. Please inform us in advance for the best experience.

Do you have meeting and event facilities?

We offer state-of-the-art meeting rooms, ballrooms, and outdoor venues for events from 10 to 500 guests. Our dedicated event planning team will assist with every detail.
