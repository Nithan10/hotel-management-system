Room Details

Our Deluxe Room offers the perfect blend of comfort and sophistication. Spanning 450 square feet, this spacious accommodation features elegant contemporary decor, a luxurious king-size bed with premium linens, and floor-to-ceiling windows offering stunning city views. Designed with both business and leisure travelers in mind, the room includes a dedicated work area with ergonomic chair, high-speed internet, and all the amenities needed for a productive stay.

Wake up to breathtaking cityscapes and enjoy your morning coffee while planning your day. The room's thoughtful design ensures maximum comfort with blackout curtains, individual climate control, and soundproofing for a peaceful night's sleep. The marble-appointed bathroom features a walk-in rain shower, luxurious bathrobes, and premium toiletries for a spa-like experience.
